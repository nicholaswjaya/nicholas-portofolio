My Arts & Sports Journey

From creative expression to competitive energy, this journey reflects the passion, discipline, and experiences that continue to shape who I am.

Chapter 01

Where Curiosity Began

2018 · Fifth Grade

My journey with music started in fifth grade when I first picked up a guitar and tried learning it on my own. After reaching a point where I felt stuck, I became even more curious about music and decided to learn classical piano with my neighbor for around four months. From there, most of my learning journey became self-taught again, including studying music theory and understanding music beyond simply playing instruments. Throughout middle school and high school, music became an important part of my life. I performed in school events and occasionally represented my school at external performances.

Chapter 02

Beyond the Court
And the Stage

2022 · Highschool

During high school, I also developed a strong interest in basketball. I actively participated in competitions and represented my school in several tournaments, including a basketball competition between regional tourism ambassadors where I represented South Tangerang. While I never pursued sports professionally, basketball taught me discipline, teamwork, consistency, and how to grow under pressure. At the same time, my involvement in music continued to evolve. I had the opportunity to become a music arranger for "Gelar Karya Kang Nong Banten," representing South Tangerang, where I learned how to combine creativity with responsibility.

Chapter 03

A Journey
Still in Progress

2025 · Present

Today, music, sports, and creative experiences continue to shape who I am. I still enjoy performing at small campus events, exploring new opportunities, and learning through every experience I take part in. Looking back, these journeys taught me far more than technical skills. They shaped my creativity, adaptability, confidence, and willingness to keep growing. And honestly, I believe this is only the beginning — there are still many experiences, challenges, and ideas I look forward to exploring in the future.
