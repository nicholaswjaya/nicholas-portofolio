My Modeling & Talent Journey

Exploring opportunities in modeling, public appearances, ambassador roles, and the creative industry.

Chapter 01

Stepping Into a
New World

2023 · First Step

My journey officially began when I participated in Abang None Kota Tangerang Selatan 2023. At first, stepping into the world of pageantry and public appearances felt completely new to me. I had little experience in public speaking, stage presence, or representing others, but I was eager to challenge myself and grow beyond my comfort zone. Through this experience, I learned that being an ambassador is not only about appearance — it’s about confidence, communication, and the ability to inspire people around you. This first step became the foundation of everything that followed in my creative and personal journey.

Chapter 02

Exploring the
Creative Industry

2024 · Growth

After entering the ambassador environment, I began exploring the creative industry more seriously. I started gaining experience in fashion shows, video talent projects, catalog modeling, and various creative productions that helped me develop both confidence and professionalism. Each opportunity introduced me to different environments and creative challenges. From working in front of cameras to performing on stage, I gradually learned how to express myself, adapt quickly, and build a stronger personal presence within the industry. This phase became an important turning point where I realized my passion for both creative talent and public representation.

— the story goes on —
Chapter 03

Representing with
Purpose

2025 · Achievement

In 2025, I was honored to be selected as Wakil II Kang Banten 2025, representing the province on a larger stage. This experience taught me that being an ambassador carries a deeper responsibility — not only to represent oneself, but also to represent culture, values, and the community with purpose and integrity. The journey strengthened my leadership, communication, and adaptability even further. Today, I continue growing as both a creative talent and tourism ambassador, combining creativity, confidence, and representation as part of my ongoing journey.
