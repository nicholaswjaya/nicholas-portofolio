01 — Featured Web · Capstone 2023

Web-Based Inventory System for Local Business

A full-stack inventory management application built as the capstone project for our Software Engineering subject. The client was a local retail business that needed a simple way to track stock, generate reports, and manage suppliers in one dashboard. My role as project lead covered system architecture, database design, and coordinating a five-person team from initial brief to final handoff. Built with PHP, MySQL, and vanilla JS, no frameworks, by choice, to keep the deployment as lean as possible for the client's shared hosting setup.

02 Data · Research 2022

Social Media Usage &
Student Productivity

A quantitative research study analyzing the correlation between social media screen time and academic performance among university students. Surveyed 120 respondents, cleaned and visualized data in Python (Pandas + Matplotlib), and presented findings to a panel. The study found a moderate negative correlation and a few surprising outliers that made the discussion very interesting.

03 UX · Design 2023

Campus Event App — UX Design Case Study

Designed a mobile app concept for discovering and RSVPing to campus events. Conducted five user interviews, built affinity maps, sketched low-fi wireframes, and iterated to a polished hi-fi prototype in Figma. Usability testing with eight participants surfaced three major pain points that shaped the final design. This project was awarded the highest mark in the cohort.
